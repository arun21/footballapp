Hugo Lloris

position: Goal Keeper

Hugo Lloris is a French professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper and is the captain of both English club Tottenham Hotspur and the French national team.

Born: December 26, 1986 (age 30 years), Nice, France
Height: 1.88 m
Weight: 78 kg
Spouse: Marine Lloris (m. 2012)
Salary: 4.56 million EUR (2012)
Current teams: Tottenham Hotspur F.C. (#1 / Goalkeeper), France national football team (#1 / Goalkeeper)
