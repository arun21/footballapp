Sergio Ramos

position: Defender

Sergio Ramos García is a Spanish professional footballer who plays for and captains both, Real Madrid and the Spain national football team. A central defender, he can also play as a right back.

Born: March 30, 1986 (age 30 years), Camas, Seville, Spain
Height: 1.83 m
Weight: 75 kg
Partner: Pilar Rubio (2012–)
Salary: 10 million EUR (2016)
Current teams: Real Madrid C.F. (#4 / Defender), Spain national football team (#15 / Defender)
